About Us

Home of Medicinal organic herbs, oils and body butters


Our product line is a combination of herbal products including traditionally used medicinal herbal infusions and teas, nutraceuticals, organic skincare oil blends and effective botanical personal hygiene products.


Our organic skincare products work with nature, not against it. As more of us are becoming conscious of our lifestyle choices, being mindful about what we’re putting on our skin is a natural and necessary next step!

Pam Oh Herbs organic skincare products have no:


 controversial chemicals

 parabens and phthalates

 synthetic colours, dyes or fragrances

 nanoparticles

And more importantly they are:


 higher levels of antioxidants

 sustainably sourced organic ingredients

 transparent manufacturing processes

 biodegradable ingredients

 minimal packaging with maximum recycled content

 protecting wildlife and biodiversity

 products that actually work

 products that really are organic and don’t contain any chemicals
